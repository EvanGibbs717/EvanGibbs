Hello and welcome to the page!  

I am Evan Gibbs, and this site is here as:  
**(1)** a showcase of what I do   
**(2)** a little hobby and repository for things I care about.  

I completed my undergraduate in Mathematics at York College of Pennsylvania under my advisor Dr. Haiping Yuan and am currently transitioning into a Ph.D. in Mathematics beginning Fall 2026. (Very) broadly, I am interested in mathematical biology (ecology). If you want to learn more about this, you can check out my [Research and Academics](/EvanGibbs/research/) and [Research Projects](/EvanGibbs/researchprojects/) tabs. In essence, I hope to develop generalizable mathematical frameworks that push forward the field of biology. I am still very much a baby in this field but am excited to keep learning!
